Angkor wat was built in the early 12th centry by king Suryvarman || (some: Sorya varman||)(1113-1150)to dedicate to vishnu in Brahmanism.This tample is beautiful and show great khmer art to the top [5]. According to George Xerxes, Angkor Wat represents the world. the central tower represents Mount Sumeru in the center of the world.the five towors represonts the summit of Mount Sumeru,the edge of the earth,and the moat surrounding the temple is the surrounding sea. Angkor Wat was orignally name Boram vishnu lok
